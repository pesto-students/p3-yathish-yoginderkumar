Rise in temperature.

As we know that each year the global temperature is rising rapidly and this is a problem of concern. But how hot it can get?

According to NASA, the average temperature around the globe back in 2013 was 14.6 degrees celsius continuing the trend of climate change and increasing temperature since 1880. The 10 warmest years have actually happened in the last 20 years. Things are heating up.

On June 26th, 2018 the town of Quriyat. Oman saw one of the hottest nights ever recorded in history. The day was pretty intense, reaching the temperature of nearly 50 degrees celsius, the night offered no respite from the heat falling to a low of only about 42.5 degrees celsius.
